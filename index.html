Robo Trek

Robo Trek is designed for those who dare to dream, create, and innovate. It's a hands-on experience that takes you from being a spectator to a true roboticist, where you learn, experiment, and discover the incredible world of robotics.

What Your Kids Learn ?

LEGO Robot Programming

LEGO Robot Programming

Kids are introduced to Robot programming using LEGO robots including WEDO and Mindstorms. They learn how to create instructions for their robots using block-based programs. Concepts like loops, conditionals, and variables are introduced.

Hands-On Robot Design

and 3D Printing

Kids design their robots, considering aesthetics, functionality, and practicality. They will then 3D Print their robot and compare with the design. They learn to think creatively and make design decisions.

Robot Motors and Sensors

Robot Motors and Sensors

LEGO Robots are equipped with smart motors and sensors (such as ultrasonic, light, and touch sensors). They learn how sensors provide feedback to the robot and how to use this information to control robot movement.

Teamwork, Collaboration, Problem Solving

Teamwork, Collaboration, Problem Solving

Some robotics challenges involve teamwork. Kids collaborate, share ideas, and work together to achieve common goals. They learn valuable communication and collaboration skills.
